| PNil

| PACase of acase

| EConst of const

| EApp of expr * expr
| EMatch of expr * case list
| ENil

and binding = bool * pattern * expr
and decl =
| DLet of binding
| DAdt of adt
and tyexpr =
| TInt
| TBool
| TString
| TList of tyexpr
| TTuple of tyexpr list
| TArrow of tyexpr * tyexpr
| TAdt of id
and adt = id * aconstr list
and program = decl list
